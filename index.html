Born in the

BLADE started as a single chair in a Brooklyn basement in 2008. No frills. Just a man, his tools, and an obsession with the perfect cut.

Today we have six master barbers, a full grooming lounge, and a waitlist that speaks for itself. The obsession hasn't changed.
